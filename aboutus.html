    Peregrine Automobiles: A Brief Story


    WHO WE ARE

    Peregrine Automobiles is a motoring company that deals in new, used and vintage cars and vehicle accessories. We take pride in offering our clients first class services and our team is always on hand to help our esteemed clients in any way we can.Peregrine Automobiles was founded in 1989 by Patrick Savage, a motoring enthusiast and mechanic who had a penchant for the now vintage type of vehicle.Our company has grown into more than just a business; it is a family, and we hope to carry on the legacy of Patrick, who is now retired but continues in the role of nominal executive chair
